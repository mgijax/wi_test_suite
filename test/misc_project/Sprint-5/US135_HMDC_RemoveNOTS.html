HMDC - Remove OMIM NOT annotations

  • US 135 - Remove OMIM NOT annotations from HMDC

Test Requirements

  1. The change to the HMDC to use the derived roll-up annotations included the OMIM NOT annotations. At this point, it is desired to remove these from the HMDC (as they were prior to this work).
  2. NOTs should be excluded from all tabs (grid, gene, disease) and all drill down pop-ups too. They also should not be factored into the count of annotations that determine the cell color.

Tests

Manual tests

  1. Verify that a disease/gene pair with only NOT OMIM annotations do not include the NOT annotations in the HMDC.

    PASS - jlewis 3/23/2015

    1. Query by gene/disease with only NOTs to the mouse gene plus an annotation to the human homolog; Prnp and 603218 (Huntington like; HDL1). Expect: Not returned on grid or gene tabs; Not displayed in PRNP/HDL1 pop-up.
    2. Query by gene/disease with only NOTs to the mouse gene and no annotation to human homolog; Tg(B2m)1Rms and 222100 (Diabetes; IDDM). Expect: Not returned on grid or gene tab.
  2. Verify that a disease/gene pair with a mix of positive and NOT OMIM annotations include only the positive annotations in the HMDC.

    PASS - jlewis 3/23/2015

    1. Query by gene/disease with a mix of annotations plus a human homolog annotation; Prnp and 600072 (FFI). Expect: Grid cell color not impacted by NOTs and not in pop-up display.
    2. Query by gene/disease with a mix of annotations and NO human homolog annotation; B2m and 222100 (Diabetes, IDDM). Expect: Grid cell color not impacted by NOTs and not in pop-up display.

Automated tests

Execute the suite of HMDC tests as part of this user story. Those tests include checks to verify that the NOT annotations are excluded. -- all NOT tests passed (meaning that NOTs are excluded from HMDC results) 3/23/2015
