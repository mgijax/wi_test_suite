Public Reports contain correct coords for markers with directly associated coordinates

  • User Story 39, Public Reports contain correct coords for markers with directly associated coordinates.
  • Reports included in test: MGI_MRK_coord.rpt (new), MGI_AllGenes.rpt, MRK_list1.rpt, MRK_list2.rpt, MGI_Coordinate.rpt, MGI_EntrezGene.rpt, MGI_GTDNA.rpt, MGI_GTGUP.rpt, MGI_GTRNA.rpt, MGI_GTdbGSS_info.rpt, MGI_IKMC_DNA_GT.rpt, MGI_IKMC_DNA_GT_info.rpt, MGI_iphone_app.rpt, MRK_ENSEMBL.rpt, MRK_List.rpt, MRK_Sequence.py, MRK_VEGA.rpt, Affy_1.0_ST_mgi.rpt, Affy_430_2.0_mgi.rpt, Affy_U74_mgi.rpt

Test Requirements

  • Reports show curator assigned coordinates
  • Reports show gene model coordinates when no curator assigned coordinates exist
  • Reports that display provider data show curator assigned provider
  • MRK_coordinate report is split into 2 new reports; MGI_gene_model_coord.rpt and MGI_MRK_coord.rpt
  • New reports created for tRNA markers, DNA segment markers, and heritable phenotypic markers
