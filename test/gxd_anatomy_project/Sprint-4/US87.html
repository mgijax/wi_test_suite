Test Requirements

  1. Display mapped EMAPS term as the structure on Assay Detail pages (replaces AD term)
  2. If an AD term is not mapped to an EMAPS term -- the annotations associated to that AD term are not displayed on the Assay Detail page
    • NOTE: during testing we found that on the Blot Assay Detail page the annotation still displays, but with a blank structure. A user story was entered -- but GXD does not find this to be an issue. Okay as is.
  3. Link to EMAPS term on Assay Detail pages (replaces link to AD)
  4. Order of annotations listed on this page should not be impacted by this change
  5. Changes apply to both types of Assay Detail pages - Blot and In situ
