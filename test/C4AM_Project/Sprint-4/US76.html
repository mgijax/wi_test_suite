US 76 - Skip cM computation when chromosomes differ

  • User Story #76 - Change cM computation to skip markers whose genetic and genomic chromosomes disagree

Test Requirements

  1. Display "syntenic" for those markers where genetic and genomic chromosome differ in the Prod WI
  2. Continue to display "syntenic" for markers with a genetic chromosome of MT, Y, or XY
  3. Continue to display no genetic location for markers with a genetic chromosome of UN.
  4. Continue to display cM locations for markers with coordinates
    • markers with gene model coordinates
    • markers with assigned coordinates
  5. Note - This story does not include Public display
